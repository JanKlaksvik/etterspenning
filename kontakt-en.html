How I can support your project

I support stressed anchoring projects with focus on standards, execution, control and documentation. The work is based on more than 16 years of practical site experience, combined with solid knowledge of relevant standards, regulations, guidance and established practice.

Technical support and advisory

  • Evaluation of anchoring solutions
  • Clarifications at the interface between design and execution
  • Practical assessments against applicable standards

Site execution support

  • Support for planning and execution of stressing and grouting work
  • Control of equipment, procedures and work sequence
  • Contribution to robust solutions that work in the field

Control and documentation

  • Setup and review/control of stressing and grouting protocols
  • Structure for traceability of equipment, calibration and completed work
  • Documentation suitable for handover and audit, reports according to applicable standards

Software and system support

  • Digital tools for registration and protocol logging (under development)
  • Structured data collection that reduces errors and omissions
  • Focus on solutions that work in practice

Knowledge sharing

  • Short technical reviews and training
  • Typical errors and deviations - and how they can be avoided
  • Support without unnecessary complexity
